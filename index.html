About Me

We often served business owners during their meetings, wedding proposal dinners, wedding receptions, and even high-ranking government officials.

My family and I moved down to Florida where I worked at a factory. Through hard work and paying attention, I became an all-around employee who they could rely on to produce results for the company in practically any positon.
